⚡ LIFE SYSTEM
Your personal life operating system — fully customized to you.
Welcome. Let's build your system.
This is not a generic app. Everything will be set by you — your goals, your habits, your schedule, your finances. Nothing is pre-defined. Takes about 5 minutes. You can change anything later.
Your Goals
Add as many goals as you want. Each goal has a current value, target, and unit. You can edit or add more anytime in Settings.
Your Daily Habits
Add habits you want to track. Pick any icon and color. You can choose if a habit is daily, weekdays-only, or custom days. Add as many as you want.
Your Finances
Set your income, add any budget categories you want to track. You can also set a debt you're paying off. All optional — add only what's relevant to you.
Budget Categories
Your Daily Schedule
Build your ideal daily schedule block by block. Add any activities — wake time, gym, work, meals, study, sleep — whatever your routine looks like.
Daily Trackers
What do you want to track every day? Set your personal targets. All are optional — only enable what matters to you.
Choose Your Modules
Which sections do you want active? You can enable/disable anytime in Settings.
